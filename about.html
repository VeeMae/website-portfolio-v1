About Me

My name is Venus Mae. A 20-something year old who loves creating things with code. I live in sunny Southern California. Among many things, I enjoy puzzles, cooking, baking pastries, and watching vlogs about life in other countries (that's what I call travelling with my eyes). With code, I hope to create accessible applications that enrich people's lives, both aesthetically and functionally.

My Journey with Code

It all started when I didn't feel creative enough as an Accounting student. I didn't really want to switch into a whole new department, so I chose Computer Information Systems, specifically emphasizing in Web Development. It was during this time that I genuinely fell in love with the creativity that came from problem solving and building applications. It would be 2 years later that I'd seriously pursue a career in Web Development, as I dabbled with Project Management for a bit. But after finding my place in the digital scene, I never looked back.
